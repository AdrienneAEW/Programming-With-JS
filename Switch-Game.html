Switch-Game App

Our version of the Game Show Network Game - Switch — Give your users a game break.

How To Play
  1. Answer 5 out of 12 questions correctly.
  2. Each correct answer moves you forward one space.
  3. Each incorrect answer move you back one space.
  4. If you reach the space marked 1 before answering 12 questions — You have won the game.
  5. If you don't reach the spaced marked 1 before answering 12 questions — You have loss the game.
  6. Have fund and good luck!
